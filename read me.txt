This package contains the code of methods used in the paper
gmmSel function:
    GMM-BIC method, y is n*p (p is dimension)
gmm_EstSel: 
    GMM penalty method, y is n*p
ep_EstSel_bic:
    EP BIC, y is n*p
ep_EstSe1: 
    EP penalty, y is n*p
EP0:
     In EP0 method file, EM_PMoEP, y is 1*n
gmrm_EstSel:
    PGMR
epr_EstSel:
    MEPR
